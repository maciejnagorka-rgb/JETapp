JTAC EVALUATION FOLDER’S TABLE OF CONTENTS :

Part I. Table of Contents

This contains a list of the sections, their contents, and quarterly review log sheet.

  • (a) Table of Contents
  • (b) 6-month Review Log

Part II. Commander’s Designation Letters

This section contains a current JTAC’s Designation Letter. Additionally, it contains any previous designation letters as applicable. In this section the JTAC Certification Letter is also to be kept.

  • (a) Current Designation Letter(s)
  • (b) Any previous Designation Letter(s) (Certification Letter), as applicable

Part III. JTAC CAS Log

This sections contains a record of all controls performed since JTAC training was initiated.

JOINT TERMINAL ATTACK CONTROLLER (JTAC) CLOSE AIR SUPPORT LOG

Part IV. Documentation of Training

This section contains documentation of all JTAC related trainings: chronological log of CAS related trainings, certificates and documents confirming additional JTAC training, including the English and Polish Air Force Phraseology Course Certificates; current continuation training sheet IAW PJMTL order; chronological log of the theoretical tests and exams results, and current gradesheets (since last JTAC evaluation), valid confirmation of the Air Force net usage approval.

  • (a) Chronological log of CAS related trainings.
  • (b) Certificates and documents confirming JTAC training.
  • (c) Valid confirmation of AIR FORCE net usage approval.
  • (d) Current continuation training sheet IAW PJMTL order.
  • (e) Chronological log of the theoretical tests and exams results, and gradesheets.

Part V. Documentation of Evaluations

This section contains documentation of all evaluations since certification. JTAC-IS and JTAC-ES store in here JTAC-VE Upgrade Qualification Sheets. Additionally, this part contains English Course certificate. Level 3 is required in the following areas: Listening, Speaking, and Reading, Level 2 is required in Writing (LSRW of 3 3 3 2) IAW STANAG 6001.

  • (a) Chronological Log of Evaluations.
  • (b) JTAC-I/E Upgrade Qualification Sheets, as applicable.
  • (c) JTAC TERMINAL ATTACK CONTROL EVALUATION forms.
  • (d) English Level 3 Course certificate.

Part VI. JTAC Formal School Diplomas

This section contains copies of ANY certificates received from attending a JTAC courses /JTAC IOQ.

Part VII. Other

This section contains copies of any other certificates and documents fulfilling national requirements (orders, national certificates, security clearances etc.).
